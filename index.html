How it works

Four things that
make it different.

Most news apps show you what happened. Verityn tells you what it means for someone with your job, in your city.

01

Why it matters to you

Every headline has a line written for your life. A finance professional in Berlin reads the same story differently than a lawyer in Mumbai. Verityn writes both versions.

02

Seven stories. Equal weight.

No lead story, no hierarchy. All seven are picked for you, each with an image, a headline, and a line that says why you should care. Scroll through them like a reel.

03

Go deeper on anything.

Tap Deep Dive on any story. Verityn writes you a full breakdown: what happened, why it happened, what different sides think, and what to watch next.

04

Follow the thread.

Big stories span days. Verityn clusters related events into threads (ECB Rates, US-China Trade, EU AI Policy) so you never lose track between updates.

Two ways to read

App for depth.
Newsletter for speed.

Your 7 stories arrive two ways. Pick the one that fits your morning, or use both.

📱

The app

Images, Deep Dive reports, Topics you can follow over time, and AI search. Everything in one place.

✉️

Same 7 stories with why-lines, straight to your inbox. Links take you to the app when you want to go deeper.
