How it works

Four things that
make it different.

01

"Why it matters to you"

Every headline comes with a line written for you. Not a generic summary — a short line explaining why this story matters to your week. A finance professional in Berlin reads the same headline differently than a trader in Mumbai. Same story, different reason to care.

02

Seven stories. Three levels.

The lead story gets the full treatment — big headline, a why-line, and a link to go deeper. The next three are "Also Today" — important but not urgent. The last three are "Worth Knowing" — a headline and one sentence. You always know what to read first.

03

Go deeper on anything.

See a headline that makes you curious? Tap "Deep Dive" and Verityn writes you a full breakdown — what happened, the background, why it matters, what to watch next, and what different sides are saying. Like asking a friend who actually reads the news.

04

Follow the thread.

Big stories don't happen in a single day. Verityn groups related events into threads — "US-China Trade", "ECB Rates", "EU Climate Policy" — so you can follow a story over days and weeks without searching for yesterday's update.
