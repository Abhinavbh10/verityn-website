How it works

Four things that
make it different.

Most news apps show you what's happening. Verityn tells you why it matters — specifically to your profession, your location, your life.

01

Why it matters to you

Every headline has a line written for your life. A finance professional in Berlin reads the same story differently than a lawyer in Mumbai. Verityn writes both versions.

02

Seven stories. Three tiers.

The lead gets the full treatment. Three are "Also Today." Three are "Worth Knowing." You always know what to read first — no scrolling to find it.

03

Go deeper on anything.

Tap Deep Dive on any story. Verityn writes you a full breakdown — situation, background, different perspectives, what to watch next. Instant briefing on demand.

04

Follow the thread.

Big stories span days. Verityn clusters related events into threads — ECB Rates, US-China Trade, EU AI Policy — so you never lose the thread between updates.

The daily brief

Your morning
starts here.

Other apps hand you a feed. Verityn reads 100+ sources — Reuters, FT, Bloomberg, the Guardian, DW, Mint, Nikkei — and writes you a brief in plain language. No algorithm optimised for outrage. No feed to scroll. Just what happened and why it matters to you.

Refreshes throughout the day

Stories stay current. Your 7pm brief looks different from your 7am brief.

Ranked by relevance, not clicks

What matters to your life — not what drives the most engagement.

Your data stays on your device

Preferences are stored locally. We don't build profiles. We don't sell data.

Situation

Why it matters to you
If you hold anything in Asia or semiconductors, analysts expect this could move your portfolio this week. Watch TSMC, ASML, and NVIDIA on Monday open.
