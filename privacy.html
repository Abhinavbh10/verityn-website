Privacy Policy
Last updated: April 2026

Verityn is a personalised news intelligence app. This Privacy Policy explains what personal data we collect, why we collect it, how we use it, and your rights under applicable data protection law including the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

1. Who We Are

Verityn is operated as an independent application. For privacy-related enquiries, contact us at privacy@verityn.app.

2. What Data We Collect

We collect only what is necessary to personalise your experience:

  • Location (country): The country you tell us you live in. Used to frame news stories for your daily life context. Stored on your device and our servers.
  • Profession: The professional category you select (e.g. Finance, Tech). Used to frame news stories for your professional context. Stored on your device and our servers.
  • Interests and topics: News topics you choose to follow. Stored on your device and our servers.
  • Push notification token: A device token generated by your operating system when you enable notifications. Used only to send your morning briefing. We do not use this for marketing.
  • Usage data: Basic information about app interactions, used only to improve the service. We do not sell this data or share it with advertisers.

We do not collect your name, email address, phone number, or payment information unless you explicitly provide them.

3. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

Under GDPR, our legal basis for processing your personal data is:

  • Legitimate interests: Personalising your news briefing based on location and profession.
  • Consent: Push notifications — you can withdraw consent at any time in Settings.
  • Contract performance: Delivering the service you signed up for.

4. Third-Party Processors

We use the following third-party services to operate Verityn. Each has been selected for their data protection standards:

  • Supabase (database) — stores your preferences and push tokens. Data Processing Agreement in place. Supabase Privacy Policy
  • Anthropic Claude (AI synthesis) — generates your why-lines. Queries include article headlines and your interests. No personal identifiers are sent. Anthropic Privacy Policy
  • OpenAI (article ranking) — generates embeddings for ranking articles. No personal identifiers are sent. OpenAI Privacy Policy
  • GNews, Guardian API, NYT API — news article sources. We fetch headlines and summaries; your personal data is not shared with these providers.
  • Unsplash — story images. No personal data shared.
  • Expo — push notification routing (iOS via APNs, Android via FCM). Your push token is routed through Expo's infrastructure.

5. Data Retention

We retain your preferences and push token for as long as you use the app. If you delete your account or request erasure, we delete your data within 30 days.

6. Your Rights (GDPR)

If you are located in the EU or UK, you have the following rights:

  • Right of access: Request a copy of all data we hold about you.
  • Right to rectification: Correct inaccurate data.
  • Right to erasure: Request deletion of all your data ("right to be forgotten").
  • Right to portability: Receive your data in a machine-readable format.
  • Right to object: Object to processing based on legitimate interests.
  • Right to withdraw consent: For push notifications, turn off in Settings at any time.

To exercise any of these rights, contact privacy@verityn.app. We will respond within 30 days.

You also have the right to lodge a complaint with your national data protection authority. In Germany: Bundesbeauftragter für den Datenschutz (BfDI).

7. Data Security

All data is transmitted over HTTPS. We use Supabase's Row Level Security (RLS) to ensure users can only access their own data. We do not store passwords.

8. Children

Verityn is not directed at children under 16. We do not knowingly collect data from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, contact us and we will delete it promptly.

9. Changes to This Policy

We will notify you of material changes to this policy through the app. The date at the top of this page reflects when it was last updated.

Privacy enquiries: privacy@verityn.app
We aim to respond within 5 business days.
